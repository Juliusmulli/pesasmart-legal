Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Effective immediately · Developed by Julius Mulli

🔒 Summary: Pesa Smart is built with your privacy first. We never sell your data, never access your M-Pesa PIN, and you control everything stored about you.

1. Who We Are

Pesa Smart is a personal finance and budgeting application developed by Julius Mulli. The app is designed to help users in Kenya and East Africa track their income, expenses, savings goals, debts, and financial health.

By using Pesa Smart, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your email address and username when you create an account.
  • Profile information: Your display name and profile photo (optional).
  • Financial data: Transaction records, savings goals, bill reminders, and debt records that you manually enter or approve.
  • SMS data (optional, with permission): If you enable Wallet Sync, we read M-Pesa and other mobile money confirmation SMSes from your device to automatically detect transactions. This is processed entirely on your device — raw SMS content is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information to improve the service.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

✅ We NEVER collect or access:

  • Your M-Pesa PIN, bank PIN, or any passwords
  • Your M-Pesa account credentials or Safaricom login
  • Personal SMS messages unrelated to mobile money
  • Contact lists, call logs, or location data
  • Any financial account credentials
  • Biometric data (fingerprint/face ID is handled by your device only)

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Pesa Smart service
  • Display your financial dashboard, transactions, and insights
  • Power the AI spending advisor with your anonymised financial patterns
  • Send bill reminder notifications (only if enabled by you)
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns

5. SMS and Mobile Money Data

⚠️ This section is important — please read carefully.

If you choose to enable the Wallet Sync feature:

  • Pesa Smart will request Android SMS read permission
  • Only SMSes from official mobile money sender IDs (e.g. MPESA, AIRTEL) are read
  • SMS content is processed locally on your device only
  • Only the extracted transaction data (amount, name, category) is saved to your account
  • Raw SMS text is never transmitted to our servers
  • You can disable this feature at any time from the Wallet Sync settings
  • Your M-Pesa PIN is never read, accessed, or requested at any point

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider with enterprise-grade security:

  • All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure hardware (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • Row Level Security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own data — it is technically impossible for one user to see another's data
  • Sessions automatically expire after periods of inactivity

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider (data is encrypted)
  • OpenRouter/AI provider: When you use the AI advisor, your anonymised financial summary (totals only, no names or raw transactions) is sent to generate advice
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights of users

8. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data in the app at any time
  • Delete transactions: Delete any transaction directly from the dashboard
  • Edit profile: Update or delete your username and profile photo
  • Disable SMS sync: Turn off wallet sync at any time from settings
  • Delete account: Contact us to permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Data export: Contact us to request a copy of your data

9. Children's Privacy

Pesa Smart is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Pesa Smart after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
